Scalar waves of orbiting star in BTZ/CFT.

By Xing-Hua Wu, Ran Li, Jun-Kun Zhao

In this paper we compute the decay rates of massless scalar wave excited by a star circularly orbiting around the BTZ black hole and that in the corresponding CFT. Two cases are discussed, one is for the non-extremal BTZ/non-chiral CFT correspondence, the other is for near-horizon-extremal BTZ/chiral 2D-CFT correspondence. We find that matches are achieved in both these two cases.
